About us

About Unearthed Crisis

Unearthed Crisis is a gripping post-apocalyptic survival game that plunges players into a world overrun by zombies. With an intense storyline, dynamic gameplay, and a rich, immersive world, Unearthed Crisis challenges players to survive against all odds.

Intense Survival Experience

Face the challenge of surviving in a world where every day is a battle for existence. Manage your health, stamina, and supplies carefully.

Form Alliances

Join forces with other survivors to increase your chances of survival. Trust is scarce, but teamwork can make the difference between life and death.

Explore and Discover

Venture into abandoned cities, forests, and secret labs. Discover the story behind the outbreak and uncover hidden secrets that could be key to humanity’s survival.
